# Customer Personas

## 1. University Dean (Dr. Sarah Jenkins)
* **Demographics**: 45-60 years old, Ph.D., manages 50+ faculty.
* **Goals**: Maintain accreditation standards, reduce faculty burnout, ensure grading fairness.
* **Challenges**: "AI Cheating" epidemic, TA budget cuts, inconsistent grading across sections.
* **Decision Factors**: **LMS Integration** (Must work with Canvas), **Data Privacy** (FERPA compliance).

## 2. High School Principal (Mr. David Torres)
* **Demographics**: 35-50 years old, M.Ed., manages limited budget.
* **Goals**: Improve standardized test scores, give teachers more time for 1:1 mentoring.
* **Challenges**: Teachers spending weekends grading, slow turnaround on student feedback.
* **Decision Factors**: **Cost** (<$5/student), **Ease of Use** (One-click grading).

## 3. Corporate Training Manager (Amanda Lee)
* **Demographics**: 30-45 years old, HR Tech Lead.
* **Goals**: Verify employee upskilling, automate compliance training assessments.
* **Challenges**: Manually checking thousands of open-ended responses for certification.
* **Decision Factors**: **Speed** (Instant results), **API Access** (Connect to HR portal).
