# Market Analysis Report

## Total Addressable Market (TAM)
* **Global AI in Education Market**: Estimated at **$5.88 Billion USD** in 2024 (Grand View Research).
* **Growth Projection**: Expected to reach **$32.27 Billion** by 2030 (CAGR of 31.2%).
* **Broader EdTech Market**: The "EdTech & Smart Classrooms" market is valued at **$197.3 Billion** in 2025.

## Serviceable Market (SAM)
* **Assessment & Grading Tools**: A key segment of the EdTech market.
* **Target Segment**: Higher Education Institutions and K-12 Districts adoption of AI tools is growing at **38% CAGR**.
* **Estimated SAM**: ~$400-800 Million (Based on 10-15% assessment software share of the AI-Ed market).

## Industry Trends
1.  **AI Integrity vs. AI Grading**: The market is split between "detecting AI" (Turnitin) and "using AI" (Gradescope/Grammarly). **SmartEvaluator-Omni bridges this gap.**
2.  **Teacher Burnout**: 44% of teachers cite grading as their most time-consuming task, driving demand for automation.
3.  **Personalized Feedback**: Shift from simple scores to detailed, qualitative feedback (which our Digital Twin provides).
